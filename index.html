About Us

Welcome to The Light Hearted Lounge, a vibrant space for students to connect and enjoy. Our mission is to be the radiant hub of positivity and creativity on campus, where students gather to celebrate the joy of laughter, forge meaningful connections, and unleash their artistic potential.

We organize various events throughout the year, from game nights to cultural festivals, creating memorable experiences for all VIT Pune students.
